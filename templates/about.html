The Beginning of RESTORE & SHINE Pressure Washing

Done right, the first time

R & S Pressure Washing came to be while I searched for a new pressure washer for home. The more I read about the different types of pumps and cleaning methods, the more fascinating it became. One day I will retire from active duty and will need a new job. But rather than work for someone else I decided small business ownership is the way to go.

In my studying and practical applications I found that the pressure is not really what cleans houses and wood. It just gets the water to where it needs to be more efficiently, whether that be 40’ above ground or 10’ away. I began to learn the chemistry behind cleaning and how acids and bases react with different stains, organic matter, and surfaces. This is when I realized that cleaning residential and commercial building exteriors and flat surfaces is fun and engaging. It is only when using science to properly formulate solutions (diluted chemical compounds) for the different problems (rust, oil stains, mold, UV damage) that said problems can be appropriately addressed, and removed. Surface cleaning compounds the problems because it does require pressure to be cleaned.

So what is the correct amount of pressure the surface can take without being damaged? That depends on what the surface is comprised of. Because it varies we have several options to increase and decrease pressure as needed. And at times the safe pressure limit for that surface is not able to remove the harsh stains. So rather than turn up the pressure and potentially damage the surface, we introduce cleaning agents.

All that is a longer way to say- I enjoy the chemistry and problem solving required to effectively clean the job site, and when complete the owners are all smiles. I get to deliver good news all day! It’s a great job.
